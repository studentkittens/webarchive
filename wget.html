Crawling Program (WGET)¶

Overview¶

Wget is used with the following params:

'wget "{user_agent}" \
      -e robots={rob} \
      --recursive \
      -l {depth} \
      --exclude-domains "{ex_domains}" \
      {custom_parms} \
      -P {folder} \
      {url}'

In custon_parms custom options from the config are inserted. They may replace options stated before. Use at your own risk.

For more information see wget(1).
